Lists & Tuples

Lists store multiple items in order. They can change (mutable).

fruits = ["apple", "banana", "cherry"]
print(fruits[0])        # apple
fruits.append("date")   # Add to end
fruits[1] = "blueberry"  # Change
for f in fruits:
    print(f)

Tuples are like lists but cannot change: coords = (10, 20)

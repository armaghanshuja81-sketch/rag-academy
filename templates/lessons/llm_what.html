What are LLMs?

LLM = Large Language Model. These are AI models trained on massive amounts of text (the entire internet, books, articles).

How They Work (Simplified)

  1. You give it text: "What is the capital of France?"
  2. The model predicts the most likely next word: "The"
  3. Then the next word: "capital"
  4. Then: "of"
  5. Until it completes the answer

Key Concepts

ConceptWhat It Means
TokensBasic unit LLMs read. ~0.75 words = 1 token
Context WindowHow much text the LLM can "see" at once
TemperatureRandomness: 0=factual, 1=creative
Training CutoffLLM only knows things up to its training date
HallucinationWhen the LLM makes things up confidently

Why RAG?

LLMs don't know YOUR data. They only know what they trained on. RAG fixes this by letting them look up your documents before answering. This is the entire reason RAG exists.
